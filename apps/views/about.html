Who is Durae?

Durae

Durae is Catholic youth service team currently serving Korean-American parishes in the New York diocese. Our mission is to encourage and inspire a spiritual life for youth, providing resources for youth to encounter God's love through the Holy Spirit


We primarily focus on serving the high school youth to encourage and to inspire their faith. Our spiritual director Fr. Lee from St. Mel's Church in flushing, NY. His vision and experieces in working with the youth provides invaluable guidance and resources.
